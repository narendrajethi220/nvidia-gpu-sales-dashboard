# NVIDIA GPU Sales Performance Dashboard
An Excel dashboard analyzing GPU sales performance across product families, 
regions, channels, and customer segments, built on a synthetic Kaggle dataset.

## Objective
NVIDIA sells GPUs across multiple families, regions and channels. This dashboard identifies which products, regions and segments drive revenue, and flags pricing or stock issues affecting performance.


## Key Questions Answered
- Which GPU families drive the most revenue?
- How has revenue trended over time?
- Which regions and channels perform strongest?
- ...

## Key Insights
1. Data Center AI is the primary revenue driver, not Gaming.
Despite Gaming being the more visible consumer product, Data Center AI GPUs generated ₹233,111K in revenue versus ₹162,950K for Consumer Gaming — roughly 30% higher. Product strategy and stock priority should lean toward this segment.

2. Revenue has grown consistently through 2024–2026, with a slight dip in the latest quarter.

3. North America and Europe together account for over half of all revenue (31% and 23%).

4. Retail/Etail and Cloud Provider are the leading sales channels.

5. Stock availability is healthy overall, but Sold Out and Backordered cases exist.

6. Segment-wise data shows Gaming as the largest customer base.
Gaming represents the majority share of transactions (68%), followed by Content Creation (16%).
